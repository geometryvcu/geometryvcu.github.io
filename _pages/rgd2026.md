<b>Lightning Talk Session</b>

The meeting will feature a lightning talk session with the aim of showcasing research by early-career participants.
Everyone is encouraged to apply to present their work at the lightning talk session. The application to contribute to this session is an optional part of the registration.

Here is the lineup of the lightning talk session:

- Avik Chakravarty (University of Pennsylvania): Globally Generated Vector Bundles on $\overline{M}_{0,n}$ from Lattice VOAs
- Daebeom	Choi (University of Pennsylvania): Contractions of $\overline{M}_{g,n}$
- Matthew Elpers (North Carolina State University): Characterizing Slopes for More Three Manifolds
- Tracy Hall: A knot-like matroid object with trivial Jones polynomial
- Ilyas Khan (Duke University): Short Time Existence of Asymptotically Conical G_2 Laplacian Flow
- Anna Paulec (Wake Forest University): Hexagonal Mosaic Knot Theory
- Shruthi Sridhar-Shapiro (University of Richmond): Stitching Pants with Stripes: Generating Nested Cobordisms
- Brandon Shapiro (University of Virginia): Hyperbolic Tilings via Category Theory
